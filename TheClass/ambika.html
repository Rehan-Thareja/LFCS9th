Neelimaa: The Frustrated One

Neelimaa is someone who often appears to be in a **Frustrated** state, dealing with emotions that sometimes overwhelm her. Her frustration is a result of various pressures and circumstances in life, making her appear distant at times. She is also **Vehli**, a term that suggests she is not particularly interested in social interactions, preferring to stay away from unnecessary engagements.

One of Neelimaa's notable traits is her dislike for children. She finds it difficult to connect with them, often feeling overwhelmed or irritated by their presence. This combination of traits makes her someone who is tough to approach, but also someone whose frustration can sometimes reveal deeper layers of her personality that others might not always understand.
