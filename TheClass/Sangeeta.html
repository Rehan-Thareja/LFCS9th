The Crying Buddha: The Sensitive Soul

The Crying Buddha is a deeply **Sensitive** individual, often wearing their heart on their sleeve and feeling the weight of the world. This sensitivity makes them highly empathetic, but also means they can easily become overwhelmed by the emotions around them.

Though **Inexperienced** in certain aspects of life, this Buddha is always eager to learn and grow, understanding that wisdom often comes with time and experience. Despite their challenges, they remain consistently **Polite** and kind, treating everyone with respect and a gentle demeanor. Their journey is one of growth, as they navigate the balance between sensitivity and the harsh realities of life.
